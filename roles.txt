What each role can do

owner

Full access in the app permission matrix.
Can read/manage organization members.
Can create/update/delete projects.
Can read/create/update/delete/assign tasks.
Can read/update assignments.
Can create/update/delete comments.

admin

Same work permissions as owner.
Can manage members, invite, remove, and change roles.
Cannot promote users to owner or modify/remove owners.

manager

Can read the organization.
Can create/update/delete projects.
Can create/update/delete tasks.
Can assign tasks.
Can read/update assignments.
Cannot manage members or change roles.

employee

Can read the organization, tasks, and assignments.
Can update status on tasks assigned to them.
Cannot create/delete projects or tasks, assign work, or manage members.

viewer

Read-only access to organization, projects, tasks, assignments, and team.
Cannot create, update, delete, assign, or manage members.

Where this is defined

Role permission matrix: src/lib/auth/permissions.ts
Task update rules for employees: src/lib/auth/task-authorization.ts
Authorization checks: src/lib/auth/authorization.ts
UI capability helpers: src/lib/auth/ui-capabilities.ts
Database enum values: src/lib/types/database.ts

Bottom line

Five distinct roles: owner, admin, manager, employee, viewer.
No member role normalization.
